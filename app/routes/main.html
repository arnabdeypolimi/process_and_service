About us

We are a non profit organization helping people in need by providing extra foods from restaurants which they generally throw away. We have a database of restaurants who provide us their extra food which we distribute between the people in need. Restaurants can register to our service to help the community. People in need and organizations(who helped others) can register our services. We have a list of API for others to use our services. API documentation can be found below.
